Cottage Pie

Description

A classic British cottage pie consists of a rich minced beef and vegetable gravy topped with creamy mashed potatoes, baked until golden. Essential ingredients include beef mince, onion, carrots, celery, beef stock, Worcestershire sauce, and potatoes. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20-30 minutes.

Ingredients

Meat Filling

  • 500g-1kg beef mince
  • 1-2 onions (chopped)
  • 2-3 carrots (diced)
  • 1-2 celery sticks (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2-3 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1-2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 300-500ml beef stock
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • thyme/bay leaves
  • oilive oil

Mash Topping

  • 1-1.5kg potatoes (peeled/chopped)
  • 50g butter
  • 50-100ml milk
  • 100g-200g cheddar cheese (optional, grated)

Steps

  1. Prepare Potatos: Boil potatoes in salted water for 15-20 minutes until tender. Drain, mash with butter and milk, and season.
  2. Cook Meat: In a large pan, brown the beef in oil. Add onions, carrots, celery, and garlic, cooking until softened (about 5–10 mins).
  3. Make Gravy: Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute. Add tomato purée, Worcestershire sauce, beef stock, and herbs. Simmer for 15-30 minutes until thick.
  4. Assemble: Pour meat filling into an ovenproof dish. Top with mashed potato and spread evenly. Sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Bake: Bake at 200°C (400°F) / 180°C fan / Gas 6 for 20–30 minutes until the top is golden and the filling is bubbling.
