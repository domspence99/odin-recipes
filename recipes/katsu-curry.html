Katsu Curry

Description

A classic Chicken Katsu Curry requires chicken breasts, flour, egg, and panko breadcrumbs for frying, paired with a savory sauce made from onions, carrots, garlic, ginger, curry powder, stock, and soy sauce or curry roux.

Ingredients

Chicken Katsu

  • Chicken: 2-4 skinless chicken breasts (or thighs).
  • Breadcrumbs: 100g-150g panko breadcrumbs.
  • Coating: 50g-100g plain flour & 1-2 eggs (beaten).
  • Oil: Vegetable, sunflower, or rapeseed oil for deep frying.

Curry Sauce

  • Aromatics: 1 large onion (finely chopped), 2 garlic cloves (crushed), 2.5cm piece of ginger (grated).
  • Vegetables: 1–2 carrots (chopped or in ribbons).
  • Spices & Flavour: 1-2 tbsp mild curry powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup.
  • Liquid/Thickener: 300ml–500ml chicken or vegetable stock, 1 tbsp plain flour (to thicken), 100ml–200ml coconut milk (optional, for creaminess).
  • Alternative: 1-2 blocks of Japanese curry roux (e.g., S&B Golden Curry or House Vermont).

Steps

  1. Prepare Chicken: Butterfly or pound chicken breasts to an even thickness, season, then dredge in flour, egg, and panko breadcrumbs.
  2. Make Sauce Base: Sauté 1 sliced onion, 1 chopped carrot, ginger, and garlic in oil until soft.
  3. Add Spices & Stock: Stir in 1-2 tbsp curry powder (plus turmeric/garam masala if desired) and 1 tbsp flour, cooking for 1 minute. Add 500ml chicken stock, 1 tbsp soy sauce, and 1 tbsp honey.
  4. Simmer & Blend: Simmer for 10-15 minutes until vegetables are very soft. Blend with a stick blender for a smooth, thick sauce.
  5. Fry Chicken: Deep-fry or shallow-fry the breaded chicken in oil at 170°C for 4-6 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Assemble: Slice the chicken, serve over rice, and generously cover with the curry sauce.
